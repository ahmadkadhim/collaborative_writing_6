He didn't know why they were there... but they were. "They," also know as the Confederate Alliance of Household Gnomes, enacted a secret mission of surveillance; fortunately, due their stout frame, he could see them perfectly and aimed his rocket launcher at them. They realized the danger they were in, and started flitting about in a nervous angry rush, desperate to get behind cover. The hallowed cries of the beasts beyond the barrier indicated to our tragic anti-hero the impending threat.
